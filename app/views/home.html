Quick fix, if you get stuck.
  1. Use it inside OBS -> with browser plugin.
  2. Never Sign Out, Once sign-in.
  3. IF Sign Out (Delete the OBS-browser cache).
  4. Go to %appdata%\obs-studio\plugin_config\obs-browser in filesystem.
  5. Delete the whole folder obs-browser.
